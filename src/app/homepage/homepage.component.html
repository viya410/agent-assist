About Agent Assist Portal

At Agent Assist, our mission is to make it easy for agents to view, access, and understand customer needs and deliver consistent solutions. Our platform is designed to bring all agents together, fostering collaboration and efficiency.

Why Choose Agent Assist Portal?

  • Ease of Access: Agents and admins can easily access relevant records for assessing customer satisfaction.
  • Efficient Management: Add new agents or delete old records with just a few clicks.
  • Real-time Collaboration: Enhance team communication and problem-solving capabilities.
